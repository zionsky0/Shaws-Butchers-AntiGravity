Allergen Information

Below you can find the full ingredient breakdowns for our products. If you have any concerns about allergens, please do not hesitate to ask a member of our team in store.

May contain traces of: milk, soya, celery, mustard, sulphur dioxide

Gluten Free option available — please ask in store

Product Ingredient Breakdowns

Sausage Seasonings

Salt, flour (calcium, iron, niacin, thiamine), rusk (wheat), spices, dextrose, preservative E221 (sulphite), sugar, diphosphates E450, triphosphates E451, polyphosphates E452.

Honey Roast Sausage

Salt, sugar, dextrose, wheat flour, calcium carbonate, iron, niacin, thiamine, stabiliser E451, yeast extract, spice extracts, onion extract, preservative E221 (sodium sulphite), antioxidant E301.

Rusk in Sausage

Dried cereal, wheat flour, salt, raising agent (sodium bicarbonate), water.

Oriental Salt & Pepper Glaze

Maltodextrin, salt (16.5%), maize starch, cracked black pepper (6.5%), ginger (2%), fennel, star anise, cinnamon, clove, sugar, chilli flakes (1%), garlic powder, colour (plain caramel, paprika extract).

Chinese Glaze

Sugar, maltodextrin, salt, starch, colours (plain caramel, ammonia caramel, paprika extract), spices (star anise, cinnamon, ginger), garlic, acid (citric acid), flavourings.

Garden Mint Glaze

Sugar, acids (citric acid, malic acid), dried glucose syrup, modified starch, salt, dried mint (3.6%), colour (copper chlorophyllin), natural flavouring.

Burger Seasoning

Rusk (wheat flour, salt, raising agent), salt, dried onion, dextrose, preservative E221 (sodium sulphite), stabilisers (diphosphates E450, triphosphates E451, polyphosphates E452), mustard powder, spice extracts, herb extracts.

Sausage Rolls

Puff pastry (wheat flour, vegetable oil (palm), water, salt), filled with pork and sausage seasoning (see sausage seasonings above).

Dijon Mustard

Water, mustard seeds, spirit vinegar, salt, citric acid, potassium metabisulphite.

Clear Honey

Pure clear honey. A blend of EU and non-EU honeys.

Got a Question?

If you have any concerns about allergens in our products, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're happy to provide detailed ingredient information for any product.
