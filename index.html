Our Family Story

Shaw's Butchers was opened by Gary Shaw, who was the pillar of Runcorn's community and was loved by so many. His son Danny then joined the fold, and Gary taught him everything he knows.

Danny now runs the shop with the help of Bev (Gary's wife), ensuring his father's legacy lives on through quality meat and genuine community service.
